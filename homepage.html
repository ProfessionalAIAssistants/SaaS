Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI Phone Receptionist service.

How does the AI receptionist sound? Do callers know it's AI?
Our AI receptionist uses advanced natural language processing and voice synthesis to sound remarkably human. In our tests, over 90% of callers couldn't tell they were speaking with an AI. You can choose from several voice profiles that match your brand's personality.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
The AI is trained to handle a wide range of common business inquiries. For situations it can't address, it will politely take a message and notify your team immediately via email or SMS so you can follow up promptly.
Can I customize what the AI receptionist says?
Absolutely! You can customize greetings, responses to common questions, and even create specific scripts for different types of calls. Our dashboard makes it easy to update these settings anytime.
How do I set up call forwarding to my team?
You can set up detailed call routing rules in our dashboard. For example, you can forward sales calls to your sales team, route technical questions to support, and send urgent matters directly to management. You can also set different rules based on time of day or day of week.
What if I need to change my plan or cancel?
You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time through your account dashboard. We don't lock you into long-term contracts, and you can export your call data if you decide to move to another solution.
Is there a limit to how many calls the AI can handle?
No! One of the biggest advantages of our AI receptionist is that it can handle unlimited concurrent calls. Whether you receive 5 or 500 calls simultaneously, each caller gets the same professional experience without wait times.
How secure is the call data?
We take security seriously. All call data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. You can set custom retention policies for call recordings and transcripts to meet your industry's regulatory requirements.
Can the AI receptionist handle different languages?
Yes! Our AI can communicate fluently in over 30 languages, including Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, and more. You can set a primary language and enable automatic language detection to serve international callers.
